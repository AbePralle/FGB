Manipulating Paths and Waypoints:

Click on "Show Zones"


Waypoints:
  Set:  Click on orange button, left-click on map to place new waypoint.
	Delete:  Right-click on existing waypoint.

Path:
  Place waypoint in current zone, adjacent zone, and destination zone.
	With waypoint button selected, right-click on the "from" zone (but
	not on a waypoint), left-click on "from" zone's waypoint, adjacent
	zone's waypoint, destination zone's waypoint, and finally destination
	zone (not on waypoint).

To show paths leaving a specific zone:
  Select zone color but not waypoint button.  Right click on an existing
	zone.
